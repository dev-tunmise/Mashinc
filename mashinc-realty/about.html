About Mashinc Realty

Pioneering excellence in Nigerian real estate development for over a decade

CEO & Founder

Abayomi Animashaun

Visionary Leader | Real Estate Pioneer | Development Expert

With over 15 years of experience in Nigerian real estate, Abayomi Animashaun has established Mashinc Realty as the country's premier development company. His vision of creating world-class properties that blend luxury with functionality has transformed skylines across major Nigerian cities.

Under his leadership, Mashinc Realty has delivered over 200 successful projects, ranging from luxury residential estates to commercial developments, consistently setting new standards in quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Leadership Philosophy

"Excellence isn't just about the buildings we create—it's about the communities we build, the lives we enhance, and the lasting legacy we leave for future generations."

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming Nigeria's most trusted real estate developer

2010

Foundation & Vision

Mashinc Realty was founded with a clear mission: to revolutionize Nigerian real estate through innovative design, quality construction, and exceptional service. Our first project in Lagos set the standard for excellence.

2015

Expansion & Growth

Expanded operations to Abuja and Port Harcourt, delivering landmark projects that became symbols of modern Nigerian architecture. Established partnerships with international design firms and contractors.

2020

Innovation & Sustainability

Pioneered green building practices in Nigeria, incorporating sustainable materials and energy-efficient systems. Launched smart home technology integration across all new developments.

2025

Future Forward

Today, we continue to push boundaries with cutting-edge designs, advanced construction techniques, and a commitment to creating spaces that enhance lives and communities across Nigeria.
