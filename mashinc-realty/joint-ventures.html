What is a Joint Venture?

A Joint Venture is a strategic partnership where two or more parties combine resources, land, capital, expertise, or networks to execute a project and share in the profits.

It is not a sale, but a win-win collaboration designed to reduce risk, maximize returns, and create lasting value.

Combined resources and expertise
