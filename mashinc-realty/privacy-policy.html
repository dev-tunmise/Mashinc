Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Mashinc Realty ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services in Nigeria.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Property preferences and search criteria
  • Financial information for property transactions
  • Identification documents as required by Nigerian law

Non-Personal Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • IP address and location data
  • Device information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide real estate services and property listings
  • To communicate about properties and market updates
  • To process transactions and legal documentation
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with Nigerian legal and regulatory requirements

4. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Property owners and developers for legitimate transactions
  • Legal professionals and government agencies as required by law
  • Third-party service providers who assist in our operations
  • Financial institutions for property financing

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

6. Your Rights

Under Nigerian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser. See our Cookie Policy for more details.
