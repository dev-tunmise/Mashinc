Cookie Policy

Last Updated: September 11, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience and allow certain features to function properly.

2. How We Use Cookies

Mashinc Realty uses cookies for various purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly:

  • Session management and user authentication
  • Security features and fraud prevention
  • Basic website functionality
  • Load balancing and performance optimization

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website:

  • Analyzing website traffic and user behavior
  • Identifying popular pages and content
  • Measuring website performance
  • Improving user experience

Functionality Cookies

These cookies enhance your browsing experience:

  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Providing personalized content
  • Saving your property search criteria
  • Language and region settings

Marketing Cookies

These cookies help us provide relevant advertisements:

  • Tracking which properties you've viewed
  • Providing relevant property recommendations
  • Measuring advertising effectiveness
  • Delivering targeted marketing content

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party services that set cookies on our website:

Google Analytics

Helps us understand website usage and improve user experience.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

Social Media Plugins

Enable sharing on social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

Each platform has its own privacy policy governing their cookies.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can choose which types of cookies to accept or reject. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.

5. Cookie Duration

We use both session and persistent cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (typically 1-2 years)

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly:

  • Property search preferences may not be saved
  • Contact forms may not work correctly
  • You may need to re-enter information frequently
  • Personalized content may not be available

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
